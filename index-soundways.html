The practice

Stillness you can feel

Soundways to Health uses the resonance of singing bowls and gong to help you settle. As tones move through the room and through you, the nervous system has a chance to slow down — and rest becomes something your body remembers how to do.

In the quiet between the notes, the body finds its own way back to calm.

No experience is needed. You simply arrive, lie back, and let the sound do the work.

Live sound

Held in resonance

From the deep swell of the gong to the shimmer of the bowls, each session is a wash of sound crafted to carry you into stillness.
