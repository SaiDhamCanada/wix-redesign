The practice

Stillness you can feel

Soundways to Health uses the resonance of sound to help you settle. As tones move through the room and through you, the nervous system has a chance to slow down — and rest becomes something your body remembers how to do.

In the quiet between the notes, the body finds its own way back to calm.

No experience is needed. You simply arrive, lie back, and let the sound do the work.

Why sound

A gentle path to rest
